What We Do

Search for and reach out to, potential/active players in Warri Tech Community ang them to stay connected with other techies/startups in the city and not operate in isolation.

Organize/facilitate tech events/projects such as Meetups, Bootcamps, Hackathons, Conferences, Free Coding programs etc. to help strengthen the tech community. We also source for grants/softloans and administer to starups in need of it.

Engage government at every level to ensure bills they pass do not hamper tech growth in anyway. Partner with like-minds to build and operate Tech hubs, R & D Centers, and Technical Colleges.
